# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Liva IPTV Stream Player** ("Application") handles your information. Our priority is to provide a secure and private media player experience.

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
**Liva IPTV Stream Player** is designed with a "Privacy First" approach.

* **No Personal Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
* **No Tracking:** We do not track your location, device ID, or your streaming history.
* **Local Storage only:** Any data you enter into the app (M3U links, Xtream Codes credentials, Favorites) is stored **locally on your device only**. We do not have access to this information.

### 2. Playlists and Content
* **User Responsibility:** The app does not provide any content. All media content is provided by the user via M3U URLs or Xtream API.
* **Data Security:** Your credentials and playlist links never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

### 3. Third-Party Services
We do not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs that harvest user data. Your privacy remains entirely under your control.
