# About Liva IPTV Stream Player

**Liva IPTV Stream Player** is a high-performance, modern media player designed for the ultimate streaming experience on iOS.

### 🚀 Key Features
* **Smart Player Engine:** Fast loading and smooth channel zapping.
* **PiP Mode:** Continue watching your favorite content while using other apps.
* **EPG Integration:** Keep track of your TV schedule with electronic program guide support.
* **Parental Control:** Secure your private playlists with a PIN code.
* **Multi-Format:** Full compatibility with M3U playlists and Xtream Codes API.

Developed with ❤️ for users who want a clean, fast, and ad-free media management tool.
