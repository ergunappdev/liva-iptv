### 💡 Frequently Asked Questions
* **The app is empty. Why?**
Liva is a player only. You must add your own M3U link or Xtream Codes login provided by your service provider.
* **Why are channels freezing?**
Playback quality depends on your internet connection and the stability of your IPTV provider's servers.
* **Is my data safe?**
Yes. All your playlist links and credentials are stored **only on your device**.
